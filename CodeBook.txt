Explanations:
1) '-X|-Y|-Z' is used to denote signal direction of 3-axial signals.
2) The set of variables that were estimated from these signals were reduced to: 
   - mean(): Mean value
   - std(): Standard deviation
   compared to the original data set   

   
Aggregations performed
-------------------
- mean() of all variables / measures for each activity and each subject
